About

Student-led rescue robotics and IoT innovation.

Incredible Tech Lab is focused on turning practical electronics, web development, AI tools, and robotics into useful safety and rescue technology prototypes.

What We Build

We create prototype systems for landslide rescue, flood rescue, fire safety, GPS tracking, missing-person search, smart monitoring dashboards, and emergency alerts.

The work combines Arduino, ESP32, ESP8266, sensors, web dashboards, robotics, camera systems, and future AI-based safety tools.

Why It Matters

Disasters need fast information. A rescue team loses time when they cannot see location, sensor data, danger level, or victim search status. These prototypes aim to make that information visible and useful.

This is not just a portfolio. It is a serious foundation for a future rescue-tech startup.

Mission

Affordable rescue technology for real emergency problems.

To build affordable rescue technology prototypes for landslides, floods, fire incidents, earthquake monitoring, missing-person tracking, and emergency response support.

01 Design rugged robotic platforms for dangerous areas where humans cannot enter safely.
02 Create IoT dashboards that show live alerts, GPS position, sensor data, and rescue status.
03 Use student-level hardware smartly to create low-cost but impressive working prototypes.
04 Build a real brand around robotics, safety, AI, web systems, and emergency innovation.
